Maison Jaja is an art project in the form of a fashion house.

Costume and Performance is the medium for us to express concerns on the intertwining of tradition, gender, heritage and culture.

The Jaja collections function as a hack, cracking open these economies and modes of production through bodily involvement and interaction.

‘Jaja’ is a Dutch interpretation of the Greek γιαγιά • (giagiá) = grandmother. Grandmothers inspire us in the making of work with an unconditional giving quality, within a domestic setting, meant to be passed on and repurposed.
