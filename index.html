Maison Jaja is a fashion house founded by Dionisis Argyropoulos Ioannou.

Costume and Performance is the medium for us to express concerns on the intertwining of tradition, gender, heritage and culture.

‘Jaja’ is a Dutch interpretation of the Greek γιαγιά • (giagiá) = grandmother.

Grandmothers inspire us in the making of work with an unconditional giving quality, within a domestic setting, meant to be passed on and repurposed.
